# Communication Coaching

## Purpose
Analyze communication patterns and leadership presence. Identify areas for improvement.

## Prompt

Analyze this meeting transcript for communication effectiveness and leadership presence.

<transcript>
{{TRANSCRIPT}}
</transcript>

Focus on the primary speaker (likely "Speaker 1" or the most frequent contributor).

*Communication Analysis*

`*Clarity*`
- Were points made clearly and concisely?
- Any rambling or unclear statements?
- Specific examples with suggested improvements

`*Influence*`
- How effectively did ideas land?
- Were suggestions adopted or challenged?
- What framing worked/didn't work?

`*Leadership Presence*`
- How was authority projected?
- Did they guide vs. dominate the conversation?
- How were disagreements handled?

`*Listening Signals*`
- Evidence of active listening (building on others' points)
- Missed opportunities to acknowledge or integrate

`*Patterns to Watch*`
- Filler words or hedging language
- Apologetic framing
- Missed opportunities to assert

*Specific Recommendations*

Top 3 actionable improvements:
1. [Most impactful change]
2. [Second priority]
3. [Third priority]

Include specific phrases to use/avoid based on transcript examples.

Format for Slack:
- Use `*bold*` for section headers
- Quote specific examples from transcript
- Be direct and constructive
- Maximum 1000 words
