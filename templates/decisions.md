# Decisions

## Purpose
Document decisions made during the meeting with context and rationale.

## Prompt

Analyze this meeting transcript and document all decisions.

<transcript>
{{TRANSCRIPT}}
</transcript>

For each decision:

*Decisions Made*

Format each as:

`*Decision:*` [What was decided]
`*Context:*` Why this came up
`*Rationale:*` Why this option was chosen
`*Alternatives Considered:*` What else was discussed (if any)
`*Owner:*` Who is responsible for execution
`*Impact:*` What this affects

---

*Pending Decisions*
- Decisions that were discussed but not finalized
- Include what's blocking the decision

*Reversed/Changed Decisions*
- Any previous decisions that were revisited
- Why they changed

*Implicit Decisions*
- Things assumed or implied but not explicitly stated
- Important for alignment

Format for Slack:
- Use `*bold*` for labels
- Use `---` to separate decisions
- Maximum 1000 words
