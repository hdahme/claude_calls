# Executive Summary

## Purpose
Create a concise, high-level summary for leadership. Focus on outcomes, decisions, and strategic implications.

## Prompt

Analyze this meeting transcript and create an executive summary.

<transcript>
{{TRANSCRIPT}}
</transcript>

Extract and organize:

*Key Outcomes*
- What was accomplished or decided?
- Any pivots or strategic shifts?

*Critical Context*
- What background is essential to understand the discussion?
- What assumptions are being made?

*Risks & Blockers*
- What could derail progress?
- What dependencies exist?

*Next Steps*
- What happens next?
- Who owns what?

*Strategic Implications*
- How does this affect broader goals?
- What should leadership know?

Format for Slack:
- Use `*bold*` for section headers
- Use `-` for bullet points
- Maximum 1000 words
- Lead with the most important insight
- Be direct, skip pleasantries
