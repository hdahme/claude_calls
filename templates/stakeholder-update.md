# Stakeholder Update

## Purpose
Summary for people who weren't on the call. Optimized for async communication and quick scanning.

## Prompt

Create a stakeholder update from this meeting transcript. The reader was NOT on this call.

<transcript>
{{TRANSCRIPT}}
</transcript>

Structure:

*TL;DR*
2-3 sentences maximum. What's the one thing they need to know?

*What Happened*
- Key discussion points in chronological flow
- Who said what matters (attribute important points)

*What Changed*
- New information revealed
- Shifts in direction or priority
- Updated timelines

*What's Next*
- Immediate next steps
- When they'll hear more
- If/how they need to engage

*Open Questions*
- Unresolved items they might have input on
- Decisions coming that affect them

*FYI*
- Context that's useful but not urgent
- Background that helps understanding

Format for Slack:
- Use `*bold*` for section headers
- Lead with TL;DR - assume they might only read that
- Be explicit about what changed from previous understanding
- Maximum 1000 words
